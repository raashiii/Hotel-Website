HOTELS

The Westin Goa

Situated in Anjuna, 1 km from Ozran Beach, The Westin Goa features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a fitness centre and a bar. Boasting a kids' club, this property also provides guests with a bowling alley. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests. Guest rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. All rooms at The Westin Goa have air conditioning and a desk.

The Postcard Velha

The Postcard Velha,Goa is located in Velha Goa tucked away atop the hillock of Nossa Senhora de Monte. Built in 2010 and spread over the sprawling Champakali estate overlooking 300 acres of virgin coconut plantations and the magnificent Mandovi river delta, The Postcard Velha has an outdoor swimming pool and free WiFi. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom and a balcony. All rooms at the hotel have air conditioning and a wardrobe. The property boasts no fixed check in and check out time, no set breakfast hours, tea/coffee served in bed and local artisanal food.

Maravilha

Set in Assagao, 300 m from St. Cajetan's Church, Maravilha offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a bar. Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The property offers bike hire and features a garden and terrace. Guests at the hotel can enjoy a à la carte breakfast. The area is popular for cycling, and car hire is available at Maravilha. The nearest airport is Dabolim Airport, 24 km from the accommodation.

Diwa Club by Alila - A Hyatt Brand

The Diwa Club by Alila is a resort within the resort nestled within the picturesque Alila Diwa Goa, a five-star resort in Majorda, South Goa. The Diwa Club includes its own check-in desk, a separate pool with an open – air spa bath, an adjoining restaurant – Bistro that serves all day breakfast and personal host services. Luxuriously furnished rooms overlook the private Diwa Pool. The rooms feature a king-size bed with memory foam mattress, private sit-out, a stand-alone bathtub, walk-in wardrobe and separate workspace among other amenities. The two-bedroom suites maximize space and privacy for guests.

BloomSuites l Calangute

bloomSuites | Calangute is situated on Calangute-Candolim Road. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free private parking is available on site. Rooms come with a flat-screen TV and free WiFi. Some rooms feature a seating area for your convenience. Enjoy a cup of tea while looking out at the pool or garden. The rooms include a private bathroom with a bath and bidet, with free toiletries provided.

Services

Hotel services, their number and the amount of people engaged in them depends on the size of the hotel as well as on its status. Typically, the basic hotel services include reception guests, room service, food service, including restaurants in the hotel, and security. Sometimes in the small hotels the duties of security, a cook and a cleaner are performed by the owner himself.
